Real-World Scale

How industry leaders leverage Kafka for billion-scale events.

LinkedIn

The creators of Kafka. They solved the "N-squared" connectivity problem where every service had to talk to every other service. Now, they process over 7 trillion messages/day through a unified event log.

Netflix

Uses Kafka for real-time content recommendations. As you watch, events are streamed into Kafka and processed in sub-seconds to update your "Continue Watching" and suggested lists instantly.

Uber

Implemented Change Data Capture (CDC). Any update to a house listing in the database is pushed to Kafka, which then triggers an immediate search index update in Elasticsearch.

System Design Masterclass

Advanced Scenarios for Senior Engineer Interviews

High Availability & Durability

Interview Scenario

Design a system for a payment gateway that ensures zero data loss even if an entire Availability Zone (AZ) fails.

Architectural Fix

Multi-AZ ISR Pattern

Set replication.factor=3, min.insync.replicas=2, and acks=all. Disable unclean.leader.election.

"This ensures at least 2 copies exist across AZs before an ACK is sent back to the producer."

Handling Traffic Spikes (Parallelism)

Interview Scenario

Your consumer lag is growing during a flash sale. You have 10 partitions and 10 consumers, but CPU is at 100%.

Architectural Fix

Horizontal Partition Scaling

Scale partitions to 50 and add 40 more consumers. Use StickyAssignor to minimize rebalance impact.

"Kafka consumption parallelism is limited by partition count. 1 Partition = Max 1 Consumer."
